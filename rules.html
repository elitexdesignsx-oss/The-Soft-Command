Expectations & Boundaries

Application Conduct

Applications are reviewed for manners, patience, clarity, and self-awareness.

Restricted Behavior

  • Repeated messages
  • Demands for immediate attention
  • Push boundaries
  • Entitlement to time or access
  • Manipulation, pressure, or guilt tactics

Disrespect ends consideration.

Access Signals

  • Consistency
  • Composure
  • Considered effort
  • Self-control

Arrogance, desperation, and performance are not access signals.

Consent & Boundaries

All interactions are consensual between adults. A boundary is final once stated.

Rules of Engagement

Availability

  • Responses are not guaranteed instantly.
  • Availability varies by schedule and fit.
  • Requests for constant access reduce eligibility.

Patience and restraint are part of the standard.

Privacy & Discretion

All communication and content are private unless explicit permission is given.

  • No screenshots
  • No reposting messages
  • No sharing private content
  • No attempting to find personal information
  • No involving third parties

Discretion is expected at all times.

Emotional Scope

Know the difference between fantasy, connection, and reality.

This service is not therapy, crisis support, or emotional dependency training.

Self-awareness is important.

Quality of Effort

Strong applications show consistency, composure, honesty, and attention to detail.

Low-effort applications are unlikely to be accepted.

Access Review

The Soft Command may decline, pause, or end any interaction that does not fit the standard.

Access is selective, not automatic.

Before Applying

Continuing to the application means you understand the access standard, privacy expectations, boundaries, and selective review process.
